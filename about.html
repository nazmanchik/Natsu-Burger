Company History

Natsu Burger was founded in 1978 by two local kids - Jack and Ryan Ilusiki. With the guidance of their father, Russel Ilusiki, the company was born and established on King Ave Seattle.

Rather than create a chain or a franshise, the founders decided to refine their menu and source ingredients from local and sustainable sources. Today Natsu Burger leads the industry in sustainable foods, and will continue to grow on this front

Company History

Jack and Ryan Ilusiki are two natives of the PNW who grew up right here in Seattle WA. However, today Natsu is much more than a two man operation

Today Natsu burger employs over 67 locals who come from all walkes of life. While the company continues to be run by Jack and Ryan, the company founders say they are open to being purshased by a collective of employees. Jack states "We will always remain true to our foundation. The only way the company will be sold, is to our own employees who have a vested intrest in our goals and our future."
