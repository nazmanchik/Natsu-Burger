Our Burgers

Our burgers are not your everyday average burgers. Not even close.

We source all of our ingredients from sustainable, organic, and local sources. Our meat, patties, garnish, and even sauces are all local. Nothing is ever frozen, nor will it ever be.

We seek only to provide our consumers and customers the best available, no matter the cost.

We have been a local company for the past two decades, and will continue to serve this community proudly. We are not a chain, a corporation, or a franchise... far from it.

We are proud of this community, this region, and this side of the world. And on that note, the food we serve at Natsu Burger is a perfect representation of that. A perfect representation of the PNW.

It is also important to note that we do not only serve burgers. We have a menu that varies seasonally and continues our tradition of only sourcing local, sustainable, and organic foods.
